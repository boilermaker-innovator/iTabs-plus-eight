About these estimators

💡 What is this?

These are ballpark estimators — not exact quotes. They help you understand rough costs and build a detailed spec for the job. When you're ready, share your estimate directly with any local tradie to get actual quotes faster.

🔒 No sign-up, no selling your data

We don't collect your info unless you choose to share it. No account needed. No ads. Just useful tools.

🛠️ Are you a tradie?

Want a branded version of these estimators for your business? The engine is open source and free. Check out the GitHub repo or get in touch.

The Problem Everyone Has

I'm Jon, a boilermaker from Perth. I noticed something: complex information stops people everywhere.

When you're reading about government policy, buying technical products, or learning something new - you hit a confusing term and give up. You either waste time searching Google, or you just move on without understanding.

iTabs solves this simply: a blue "i" button next to confusing terms. Click it, get a clear explanation. That's it.

The Breakthrough: I was trying to build custom solutions for specific customers. Then I realized - why not just create a free generator that makes the code, and let anyone use it however they want?

Why This Makes Sense

Plays to My Strengths

I'm a "big picture" guy. I see the vision, build prototypes, make connections. But I don't want to run infrastructure or do custom development for clients.

Free generator platform works because:

  • Build once, everyone benefits
  • No support burden (users own their code)
  • No infrastructure costs (users host their own)
  • Focus on improving the generator, not managing accounts

Copying Actually Helps

Most businesses fight copycats. For iTabs, more copies means more success:

  • More websites get clearer information
  • iTabs becomes standard, like QR codes
  • Familiarity creates more adoption
  • Compete on quality, not scarcity

Like QR codes: They're everywhere because anyone can generate them freely. If they needed accounts and subscriptions, nobody would use them.

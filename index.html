💡 How it works

Tap through the options for your trade job. Each choice builds an itemised spec — the exact details a tradie needs to quote you. Send the same spec to multiple tradies and compare quotes that actually match. Tap the ⓘ buttons inside each estimator for tips and local info.

iTabs is open source. One universal engine powers every estimator — you just write a config. Want one for your business? Visit GitHub or learn more.

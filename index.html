The Problem iTabs Solves

×

People abandon purchases, donations, and decisions because they can't quickly understand what they're looking at.

üõí E-Commerce Problem

Customer sees "NWP-B4C brake pads" - no idea if it fits their car. They leave without buying. Lost sale.

‚ù§Ô∏è Crowdfunding Problem

Beautiful campaign photo, but donors can't quickly see where money goes or why it's urgent. Campaign fails to reach goal.

üè† Real Estate Problem

Gorgeous property photos, but buyers need room sizes, renovation details, neighborhood info before booking viewing. Wasted time for everyone.

üíº Portfolio Problem

Creative work looks great, but without context about client brief, approach, and results, it's just pretty pictures. No credibility.

The Current Solution Doesn't Work:

Option 1: Put all info on the image ‚Üí Cluttered, ugly, nobody reads it

Option 2: Put info in description below ‚Üí People don't scroll, they click away

Option 3: Make people click through to another page ‚Üí Too many steps, they abandon

iTabs Solution: Keep images beautiful. Hide details behind one simple "i" button. Information appears instantly when needed, stays hidden when not.

The Problem

Getting a trade quote shouldn't take a week

Right now, getting quoted on a trade job is slow and frustrating for everyone involved.

😐

Customers don't know the options

You need a fence but don't know Colorbond from aluminium slats. So you say "just a fence" and hope for the best.

📞

Tradies play 20 questions

What type? How long? What height? Access? Removal? The tradie has to drag every detail out, one phone call or text at a time.

🍎🍊

Quotes don't compare

One tradie quotes removal included, another doesn't. One includes gates, another charges extra. You can't compare apples to apples.

⏳

Everyone wastes time

A job that could've been quoted from an email takes three calls, a site visit, and a week of back-and-forth.

How It Works

Complete job details in 60 seconds

Tap through the options, learn as you go, send the same detailed spec to any tradie.

1

Tap through your options

Choose materials, sizes, extras, and site conditions. Each option is explained in plain language so you know what you're picking and why it matters.

2

Send complete specs

Every choice you make builds an itemised job spec — the exact same details a tradie needs to give you a price. Share it via WhatsApp, SMS, email, or just copy it.

3

Get quoted faster

Tradies get everything upfront. No chasing details. Many jobs can be quoted straight from the spec without a site visit. Same spec to multiple tradies means you can actually compare quotes.

The Differentiator

The Bigger Picture

Where this is going

iTabs started with fencing estimators in Perth. The vision is bigger.

📂

One engine, infinite trades

Fencing, electrical, plumbing, paving, landscaping, painting — each estimator is just a config file. Adding a new trade is adding a new config, not building new software.

🌏

Open source — copy it, improve it

The code is on GitHub. There's a SKILL.md file that lets any developer or AI generate new trade estimators from scratch. The more people use it, the better it gets.

🤖

Ready for AI agents

As AI starts helping people plan and price home improvements, iTabs' structured data means agents can read, compare, and present options without scraping messy websites.

🏘️

Property managers & real estate

Anyone who coordinates trades regularly — rental maintenance, property prep for sale, renovation projects — can use iTabs to spec jobs consistently and get faster turnaround.
